# FluentGe Competitor Analysis (Feb 21, 2026)

## Key Finding: ALMOST NO DIRECT COMPETITION

Searched Google Play Store for "English learning Georgian" — found ZERO serious competitors in our exact niche (English learning app FOR Georgians, IN Georgian).

## What Exists

### Direct Competitor
- **"Learn English Through Georgian"** by Skyraan Technologies
  - Basic quiz app (birds, animals, fruits, flowers)
  - Multiple choice + timer
  - No rating shown, likely very few users
  - **Our advantage:** FluentGe has 90 decks, 48 learning modes, phrasebook, podcast, grammar — 10x more comprehensive

### Indirect Competitors (Generic English Learning)
- **Duolingo** — No Georgian language support! (huge gap we fill)
- **Drops** — Has Georgian but as "learn Georgian" not "learn English for Georgians"
- **Ling** — Teaches Georgian to foreigners, 4.7 rating
- **50 Languages** — Generic, not Georgian-focused

### "Learn Georgian" Apps (NOT our competitors)
- Kartulingo, MTL Learn Georgian, Learn Georgian Pro, AniBani
- These teach Georgian TO foreigners, not English TO Georgians

## Our Competitive Advantages
1. **Only comprehensive English learning app in Georgian** — Duolingo doesn't support Georgian!
2. **90 word categories** with Georgian translations (vs competitor's ~10 categories)
3. **48 learning modes** (games, quizzes, flashcards, wordle, crossword, etc.)
4. **1,700+ phrase phrasebook** with audio
5. **21 podcast episodes** with real dialogues
6. **38 grammar lessons** (A1→B2)
7. **PWA** — works offline, installable
8. **Free** — no paywall for basic features
9. **Culturally relevant** — decks for MMA, gaming, driving in Georgia, etc.

## Market Size (Estimated)
- Georgia population: ~3.7 million
- English learners in Georgia: ~500K-1M (English is mandatory in schools)
- Smartphone users: ~2.5M
- Potential market: 200K-500K users
